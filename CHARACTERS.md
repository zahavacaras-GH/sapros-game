> The canonical physical descriptions for every character in Sapros.
> These are the **locked references** pasted into every image prompt for that character. They ensure visual consistency across hundreds of images generated by Cowork (or anyone else).
>
> If you ever change one of these — different ethnicity, age, defining feature — update this file *and* the matching entries in:

## MIKA — protagonist

**Age range:** 15 → 19 (the game spans four years; older variants appear in some endings)

**Permanent features:**
- Slim build (5'9" by age 19)
- Olive-tan skin
- Dark brown hair, kept short and increasingly neat as he climbs
- Sharp brown eyes with a slight downturn — gives him a permanent half-tired, half-watchful look
- Small scar on his left jaw (childhood)

**Wardrobe by age:**

| Age | Rank | Wardrobe |
|---|---|---|
| 15 | Civilian | Scrappy civilian clothes, slightly too-big jacket |
| 16 | Thebes / Skiritai | Black Skiritai field uniform with silver shoulder insignia, high collar |
| 17 | Achaean | Black Achaean uniform with gold trim, side-arm at hip, polished boots |
| 18 | Agema | Formal grey Agema coat, two rows of three medals, black gloves |
| 19 | Hetairoi | Tailored white-and-gold Hetairoi uniform, silver collar pin, no visible weapons |
| 21–30 | Council head | White-and-gold with additional gold trim signaling Council leadership; sharper jawline; slight grey at temples emerging by mid-20s; slight wear at eye corners |

**Personality cue (for facial expression):** Charming, resilient, manipulative. Quiet rage hidden well.

**Reference sheet (for prompts):**

> MIKA (age N): slim build, olive-tan skin, dark brown hair worn short, sharp brown eyes with a slight downturn giving him a permanent half-tired, half-watchful look, small scar on his left jaw. Wearing [age-appropriate uniform from table above]. [Age-specific note: e.g., "looks scared but trying to seem unbothered" for 15, "always-deciding mouth" for 19+].

## JASON — Achaean mentor, true believer

**Age:** Mid-30s (around 32)

**Permanent features (must always appear):**
- Lean and hard
- Pale weathered skin
- Close-cropped blond-grey hair
- Steel-grey eyes

**Reference sheet:**

> JASON (age ~32): lean and hard, pale weathered skin, close-cropped blond-grey hair, steel-grey eyes, OLD BURN SCAR on the right side of his neck starting under the jaw and disappearing into his collar. Wearing his black Achaean uniform with gold trim. Calm, watchful, the bearing of a man who has never doubted himself.

## Notes for image generation

1. **Always paste the full reference sheet** into the prompt for any image featuring that character. Don't paraphrase.
2. **Specify the age** every time — Mika at 15 looks meaningfully different from Mika at 19 looks different from Mika at 25 (Worse-than-Vespera ending).
3. **For Vespera specifically:** the hair style must vary across scenes. Each prompt should specify which style. Don't ever let two adjacent scenes have her in the same hair.
4. **For Sophie post-age-18:** always include "scar through left eyebrow." It's small but consistency matters.
5. **For Jason:** the burn scar on the right neck is non-negotiable. If a generation hides or omits it, regenerate.
6. **For Kaden:** his gauntness should progress visibly with age. A teen-healthy Kaden at 18 looks wrong.
7. **All characters render in:** cinematic photoreal style, naturalistic lighting, modern surveillance-dystopia setting. No fantasy, no anime, no painterly look.
